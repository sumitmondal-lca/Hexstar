Apply for Internship

At Agnirath Aerospace and Defence Research Private Limited, we offer exciting internship opportunities for college students who are passionate about space technology, robotics, AI, and engineering.

We provide students the chance to work with real-life space technologies, such as satellites, rovers, drones, and advanced aerospace systems. Our interns collaborate directly with our professional engineers and researchers, gaining hands-on experience and learning how real-world projects are developed and executed.

This opportunity not only enhances technical skills but also boosts career prospects by offering exposure to actual industry practices. Interns can also showcase their projects in our company’s seminars and workshops, building their confidence and professional network.

If you are a college student and want to work on real space technologies, apply now and be a part of the future of space exploration!
