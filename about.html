About Us

Agnirath Aerospace and Defence Research Private Limited is a Kolkata-based space research organization dedicated to advancing space technology for Bengal as well as for India. Established in January 2024, our mission is to make Bengal a prominent hub for space research and technology development. We are proud to be a registered Space Tutor of ISRO and are officially registered under the Ministry of Corporate Affairs, Government of India.

At Agnirath Aerospace, we are deeply committed to promoting space-based education and awareness. We conduct webinars, seminars, workshops, and masterclasses across various fields such as space technology, astronomy, remote sensing, satellite technology, aerospace engineering, robotics, navigation and positioning, and rocket science. Through these initiatives, we aim to nurture the next generation of scientists, engineers, and explorers.

Beyond education, we actively design and develop advanced space technologies. Our work includes:

  • Designing and building satellites for Earth observation and communication.
  • Developing rovers and drones for space and terrestrial applications.
  • Innovating new solutions for Earth and space observation technologies.

We are also in the early stages of developing suborbital sounding rockets. These rockets will serve educational, research, and commercial purposes, enabling students, institutions, and private organizations to access space in a cost-effective way.

Currently, Agnirath Aerospace is focused on expanding its team to accelerate its growth. We are offering opportunities for students and young professionals to join us, with the vision of building a stronger workforce for the future.

Our dream is bold: to make Kolkata the space tech capital of India and to lead the nation toward new frontiers in space exploration and technology innovation.

Together, let's reach for the stars!
