Agnirath where innovation, education, and Science come together to shape the future of space

Agnirath Aerospace and Defence Research Private Limited is a Kolkata-based space research organization dedicated to advancing space technology for Bengal as well as for India. Established in January 2024, our mission is to make Bengal a prominent hub for space research and technology development. We are proud to be a registered Space Tutor of ISRO and are officially registered under the Ministry of Corporate Affairs, Government of India.

Internship

At Agnirath Aerospace and Defence Research Private Limited, we believe in nurturing the next generation of space technology innovators. We offer exciting internship opportunities for students passionate about aerospace technology, space science, robotics, AI, and engineering.

As an intern with us, you will work alongside our experienced engineers, scientists, and professionals, gaining hands-on experience in real-world projects such as satellite development, drone technology, rover design, and more. This is an excellent opportunity to build your skills, expand your knowledge, and gain valuable industry exposure.

We are committed to providing an enriching learning environment where interns can contribute to cutting-edge technology and innovations. Interns will have the chance to showcase their work in company workshops and seminars, boosting their career prospects and confidence.
